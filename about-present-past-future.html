About Present, Past and Future

Present

Regular, schedule, obligation, responsibility

V(s) - do/does - present simple - обычно, я читаю

Right now, this period

am/is/are + Ving - present continuous - я сейчас читаю

Past

Narrative, Activity is finished, time phrase, but not always

Ved/V2 - did - past simple - я прочитал

Narrative, The events taking place around the time of the story

was/were + Ving - past continuous - я читал

Narrative, An action that happened before something else we already talked about

had + Ved/V3 - past perfect - я читал уже тогда

Совершенный результат, подчеркивается результат

Activity is still taking place or affecting the present situation

have/has + Ved/V3 - present perfect - я закончил читать

Совершенный результат, подчеркивается продолжительность действия

Activity is still taking place or affecting the present situation

have/has + been + Ving - present perfect continuous - часто с вопросом how long - я читаю уже два года

since/for

since - shows when activity started - present perfect / present perfect continuous

for - shows the length - past simple

Future

Спонтанность, неуверенность

will + V - future simple - я прочитаю

Definite plans / Questions about plans

am/is/are + going to + V

am/is/are + Ving - present continuous

Obligation

have to + V

Less certain plans

will + probably/possibly + V

probably won`t + V

be thinking of + Ving

might/may (possibly) (not) + V
